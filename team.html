Our Team:

This project started off as a curiousity to understand maternal mortality. We're all students from The Knowledge Society - a human accelorator program for teenagers. After weeks of learning about the challenges of pregnant women in Africa, we got inspired to contribute to helping solve this problem. With mentorship from experts, we've worked to develop EMM. Previous to this project, we consulted for companies like Sidewalk Labs and Kidogo; made appearances on global stages like Web Summit and Microsoft Ready; worked on research projects in regenerative medicine, cultured meat & deep tech - all before the age of 17. As dedicated, hardworking and curious students, our goal is to help build an initiative powered by the community. Real, long-lasting change comes from the ground-up. We want to help shape a future where women survive and thrive - and communities have great healthcare.
